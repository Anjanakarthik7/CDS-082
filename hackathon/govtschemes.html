Government Schemes

Here you can get to know about the recent active plans for farmers from Government

1.Pradhan Mantri Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (PM-RKVY)

This scheme aims to improve agricultural extension and deliver better technology and practices to farmers.

2.Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

This scheme provides an income support of Rs. 6,000 per year to all farmer families in the country.

3.Krishi UDAN 2.0

This scheme will be implemented at 53 airports across the country to benefit farmers, freight forwarders, and airlines.

4.National Mission on Agricultural Extension and Technology (NMAET)

This scheme aims to improve agricultural extension and deliver better technology and practices to farmers.

5.Kisan Credit Card

This scheme provides information on the Kisan Credit Card for animal husbandry and fisheries farmers.
